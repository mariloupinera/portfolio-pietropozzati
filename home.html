Negri Fratelli s.r.l.

Since the beginning of 2021, I'm in charge of communication for one of the leading company in the Italian market of ball valves for natural gas. Since it's not easy to differentiate valves-actually, they all look the same in people's eyes-I was asked to find a way to communicate their superior quality. So I increased the company's social media presence, creating posts mostly about its history and positioning inside and outside the country.

I want to give a taste of what designing value content means to me. In April, it was Earth Day, and I wanted to take the opportunity to celebrate it with the company's social media profile. So basically, I replaced the sphere valve in the company's logo with earth and the following caption: "Take care of our most important sphere". That post made a lot of noise and was appreciated. Who said simpler things are less effective? I also made a campaign to celebrate the 101st anniversary of the company.

Key Hospitality

On the 2nd of September 2021, I arrived in Amsterdam. It took me only two weeks to find a job. I applied for a social media based role, at the same time, they needed a receptionist for their hotels. I saw an opportunity and offered my help to do both, developing a social media calendar and creating contents for social media while entertaining guests at the reception. It is a demanding experience that doubtlessly allows me to grow both personally and professionally.

We are talking about a Young and International Company who wants to reach young people as their audience, mainly students, coming from all over the world to visit the city. So I came up with the International Puzzle Day idea, I created this post with the following caption to communicate our core values to the audience: "In Key Hospitality we are like puzzle: An international team in which every single piece counts, and it is relevant to build up a bigger picture."
