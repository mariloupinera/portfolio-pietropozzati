Negri Fratelli s.r.l.

Since the beginning of 2021, I'm in charge of communication for one of the leading company in the Italian market of ball valves for natural gas. Since it's not easy to differentiate valves-actually, they all look the same in people's eyes-I was asked to find a way to communicate their superior quality. So I increased the company's social media presence, creating posts mostly about its history and positioning inside and outside the country.

Negri Fratelli s.r.l.

I want to give a taste of what designing value content means to me. In April, it was Earth Day, and I wanted to take the opportunity to celebrate it with the company's social media profile. So basically, I replaced the sphere valve in the company's logo with earth and the following caption: "Take care of our most important sphere". That post made a lot of noise and was appreciated. Who said simpler things are less effective? I also made a campaign to celebrate the 101st anniversary of the company, check it out: facebook.com/negrifratelli

Key Hospitality

On September 2nd, 2021, I arrived in Amsterdam, and it took me only two weeks to find a job. Actually I applied for a social media based role, but they also needed a receptionist for their hotels. So I helped them by both developing a social media calendar and creating some content for social media and dealing with customers at the desk. It is a demanding experience that doubtlessly allows me to grow both professionally and as a man.

International Puzzle Day!
